Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 3 cups macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 cups shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ÂºF.

  2. Cook macaroni in large pot for 7 minutes. Strain.

  3. Melt butter in a saucepan. Add flour and salt. Stir with a rubber spatula until bubbling. Add milk and stir constantly until the mixture thickens. Gradually stir in handfuls of cheese until the mixture is smooth and bubbling.

  4. Mix cheese and macaroni and pour into a large greased pan.

  5. Bake for 20 minutes.
